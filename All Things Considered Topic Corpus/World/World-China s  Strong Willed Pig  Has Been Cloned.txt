 There is news today of a pig.   In particular a 330-pound pig who became a Chinese national hero. His name is Zhu Jian Qiang which translates to strong-willed pig. He got his name after he survived the 8.0 magnitude earthquake in Sichuan Province in 2008.   Six piglets were made with his DNA. Reports say the pigs will be sent off in pairs to live in a museum and a genetic institute. Scientists want to know what made this strong-willed pig so strong-willed.
