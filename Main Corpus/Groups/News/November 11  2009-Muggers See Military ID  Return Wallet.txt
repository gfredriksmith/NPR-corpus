 Good morning. Im Steve Inskeep with a report of honor among thieves. A University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee student was mugged yesterday morning. Four men stopped him at gunpoint. They grabbed his wallet. But one robber took a moment to look through the wallet and found a military ID. The victim was an Army reservist. The muggers apologized thanked the reservist for his service returned the wallet and gave the man a fist bump. On this Veterans Day November 11th its MORNING EDITION.
