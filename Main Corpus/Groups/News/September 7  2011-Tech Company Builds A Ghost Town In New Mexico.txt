 Good morning. I am Steve Inskeep. New Mexico wants to make itself a home for new technology. So it is welcoming a tech company that wants to build an entire new city  with no residents. The company plans a 20-square-mile model metropolis. This new town would be designed to test everything from renewable energy to intelligent traffic systems to new wireless networks.
