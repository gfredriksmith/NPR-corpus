 Good morning. I am David Greene. A Connecticut couple expecting a baby boy soon could not decide whether to name their son Jackson or Logan. So according to the New Haven Register they decided to take a poll of customers at Starbucks. More than 1800 votes were cast including some write-in suggestions like Obama and Jebediah. Even with all the help though they still could not choose. So they went with both options and decided to name their son Logan Jackson. You are listening to MORNING EDITION.
