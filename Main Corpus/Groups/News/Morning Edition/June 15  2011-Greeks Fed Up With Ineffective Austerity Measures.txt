 Unidentified Man   Georgia Nikopoulou says the protest has given many despairing Greeks some hope. She is a 28-year-old marine biologist who speaks four languages but can only find work at a shop. Because business is bad she says she has not gotten paid since January. Many of her friends have given up she says.  Most of them have left the country because there is no future for us here.
