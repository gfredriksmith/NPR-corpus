 Good morning. I am Renee Montagne. In China inventing stuff is a very good way of spending time in jail. That is because a law allows certain prison terms to be commuted for convicts who come up with serious technical innovations which The Beijing Youth Daily reports has led to a brisk business in getting-out-of-jail patents. For as little as 1100 wealthier inmates are buying patents leading one Weibo-it  to dub prisons China's Nobel Prize centers. It is MORNING EDITION.
