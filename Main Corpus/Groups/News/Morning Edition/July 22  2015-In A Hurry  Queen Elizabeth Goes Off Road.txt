 Good morning. I am Renee Montagne. Being queen apparently does not exempt one from being what we all are at times an impatient driver. Queen Elizabeth was driving to church through the park leading out of Windsor Castle and she just could not pause for a family out for a Sunday stroll blocking her way. So reports the Telegraph the queen veered onto the grass. Though perhaps unlike the rest of us the queen smiled and waved as she passed the startled family. It is MORNING EDITION.
