 Good morning I am Renee Montagne. Tasmanian devils are back in LA. After 20 years without a devil the LA Zoo has welcomed a pair of the marsupials. Generations of American children met Tasmanian devils through Looney Tunes where Taz was a ferocious whirling beast. The real ones do bear their sharp teeth to achieve a devilish look. In fact they are endangered and joined the LA Zoo in part to generate support for the creatures. It is MORNING EDITION.
