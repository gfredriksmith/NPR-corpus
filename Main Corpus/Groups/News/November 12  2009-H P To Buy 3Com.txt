 NPR's business news starts with the tech world shifting battle lines.   Hewlett-Packard says it is buying the network equipment maker 3Com. HP is a global giant in personal computers and printers. With this deal HP would also sell more of the stuff that connects computers. Internet networking gear is a business long been ruled by Cisco. Now HP is going head to head with Cisco. Then again Cisco has been encroaching on HP's territory in recent years by making deals with companies in competition with Hewlett-Packard products.
