 Earlier today Patricia Llodra who is the first selectman of Newtown spoke about how her community is coping. Here is part of what she had to say.  Newtown has suffered a tremendous tragedy a harm that has broken our hearts. Our wound is deep because we are a close-knit community. We truly care for each other. We are coming together with love and support for those families who have suffered this terrible loss. We are a strong and caring place. We will put our arms around those families and around each other. We will find a way to heal so that all of our residents young and old will again find peace.  Newtown first selectman Patricia Llodra.
